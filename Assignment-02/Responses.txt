Briefly describe one way how data travels on the internet.-
  Information sent into the internet is broken up into data packets. These
  packets are then reassembled at the receiver's end.

What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?-
  An IP address is a unique and specific set of numbers that identifies and
  sets computers and servers apart. A DNS takes an IP and translates it into words
  and letters.

What is a cookie?-
  Cookies are files that store your preferences. Cookies can store (but are
  not limited to) language, items in a "shopping cart" for online shopping, the
  time the user enters a site.

List the three basic parts of a URL.-
  Protocol, Domain name, and path
