About This

For about two years now, I have been really getting into Dungeons and Dragons. I have been in one major campaign, which lasted about a year and a half, and multiple one-shots. The interaction between characters and the people controling them is amazing and very fun to see for yourself. One thing that I very much enjoy though are making characters that I get to play as. Going through the Player's Handbook and researching how my character is going to turn out brings me immense enjoyment. Not everyone makes characters the same way I do though, so if you would like to learn more, Youtube and Reddit are very good places to start looking if you would like some more information on making characters.
