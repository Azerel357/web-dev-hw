How to make Cinnimon Pull-aparts

Ingredients (makes two batches)

  • 1 package of Rhodes frozen dinner rolls
  • 3 sticks of butter
  • 4 tablespoons of milk
  • 4 tablespoons of light Karo Syrup
  • 3 cups of brown sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • 2 Bundt pans

Directions

  1. Thaw the frozen Rhodes dinner rolls on a cookie sheet, when 1/3 thawed,
    cut each circle of dough into 4 pieces with scissors. Let sit on cookie sheet
    until thawed and a little raised. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
  2. To make caramel sauce: Melt butter in a saucepan then add the other ingredients,
    sprinkle with cinnamon to your liking. Bring sauce to medium boil on medium
    high heat stirring intermittently x 2 min (not roaring boil). The sauce
    will look like a caramel color. Then remove from heat. Donâ€™t let it get
    thick in the saucepan or it will be hard to drop into the Bundt pan.
  3. Spray Bundt pan with non-stick spray. Use a ladle to spoon 1 layer of sauce
    in the bottom of the pan, followed by 1 layer of the cut dinner rolls.
    Cover that layer with caramel sauce followed by another layer of dough,
    repeat until you get 3-4 layers, use the last of the caramel sauce on top
    of the rolls. Let sit and rise to 2/3 to 3/4 of the pan.
  4. Bake at 350F. for 40 min. Let the pan sit for about 5 minutes then
    dump the Bundt pan upside down on a plater or round cake storage container.
  5. Enjoy! Heat rolls up in the microwave if eating after the rolls cool to
    soften the dough and caramel sauce if needed.
